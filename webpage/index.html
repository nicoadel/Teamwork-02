Course Content

Our Full Stack training is structured modularly in two parts: the front-end and the back-end. You can book them separately but best job chances you will get taking the full course.

Front-End

On the front-end development training you learn all the core technologies and how to build up a website, how to add elements such as text, images or videos. On top of that, you will also learn jQuery, Bootstrap and Angular. As a Front-end developer, you are able to set up stunning websites that are interactive and look awesome on every device (responsive). Front-end developers are hugely in-demand around the globe. more Info...

Back-End

Back-end developers work on programmed solutions that run in the background of a website or web-application. This is also called server-side programming, where the majority of brainwork takes place. For example when you list used cars on a website, the system has to read out and filter the information from a database that is stored on the server. Without back-end programming, it would not be possible. more Info...
